Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup melted butter (1 stick)
  • 1 cup sugar (brown sugar's the best)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp instant coffee (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prep
    • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
    • Line an 8x8 pan with parchment or lightly grease it
  2. Mix the wet ingredients
    • In a bowl, whisk melted butter + sugar until well combined
    • Add eggs + vanilla and whisk until smooth and glossy
  3. Add the dry ingredients
    • Add cocoa powder, flour, salt, and instant coffee (if using) to the wet mixture
    • Stir until just combined — don't overmix
  4. Bake (20-25 minutes)
    • Spread batter into the pan
    • Bake until the top is set and a toothpick comes out with moist crumbs (not wet batter)
    • Let cool 5-10 minutes so they slice cleanly
  5. Most important step
    • Make a cup of tea because you can't have brownies without it!
    • Eat your delicious brownies! ^_^
